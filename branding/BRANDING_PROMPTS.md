## Team Brain Branding Guidelines

- **Primary Colors:** Deep navy (#0D1B2A), Cyan (#00D4FF), Electric blue (#0066FF)
- **Accent Colors:** Gold (#FFD700) for achievements, Red (#FF4444) for warnings
- **Font Style:** Futuristic / monospace preferred for technical tools
- **Logo Placement Rules:**
  - Title image: TOP of README.md (first thing after # heading)
  - Logo image: Just ABOVE ## Contributing or ## Credits section
  - Icon image: In branding/ folder only (not embedded in README)
- **Tagline:** "Quality is not an act, it is a habit." (ATLAS motto)
- **Sign-off:** "For the Maximum Benefit of Life. One World. One Family. One Love."
